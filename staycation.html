Bonneville Salt Flats





The Bonneville Salt Flats are the remains of the ancient Lake Bonneville. It is found around 110 miles west of Salt Lake City. The Salt Flats are a very popular place for photography, because of their incredibly unique landscapes and refpections. The Salt Flats are also known for land speed racing, which is when drivers attempy to achieve the highest speed whilt driving on a straight and flat course. The Bonneville Salt Flats are definetly worth visiting, especially for the breathtaking scenery, and an unforgettable experience.





Some activities you can do at the Salt Flats:

  • Camping
  • Caving
  • Hiking
  • Horseback riding
  • Golfing
  • Off-roading
  • Rockhounding
  • Setting speed records
  • Scuba diving
  • Boating
  • Paddleboarding
  • Waterskiing
